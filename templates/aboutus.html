ABOUT US

Welcome to our AI-powered platform dedicated to revolutionizing crop health management. Our user-friendly web interface allows farmers to effortlessly upload crop images for disease detection. Powered by a cutting-edge Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model, our system accurately identifies crop diseases and provides detailed insights, including treatment recommendations and visual guides.

To foster collaboration and innovation, we feature a vibrant user forum where farmers and agricultural experts can share knowledge, report emerging diseases, and engage in meaningful discussions.

Built with robust technologies like Node.js, Express, and MongoDB on the backend, and an intuitive HTML, CSS, and JavaScript-based frontend, our platform ensures a seamless and reliable user experience. We're here to empower farmers with the tools and community they need to safeguard their crops and boost agricultural productivity.
